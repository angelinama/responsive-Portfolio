About Me

My name is Angelina Ma (Chinese: 马冰清). Currently, I am working as a software engineer at Amazon. I live with my boyfriend in South Lake Union, Seattle. Before moving to Seattle, I had quite a journey. I came to the States in Aug 2015. I went to Cornell University and graduated in May 2018 with masters in Chemical Engineering and Computer Science. After my graduation, at first, I lived in SF Bay Area and worked for start-up called Lime.

Outside of work, I enjoy travelling, live music, hiking, kayaking and (guilty as charged) shopping design brands. The current pandemic really affects me to go out and about tho. If you want to know me more, please use the contact link to leave a message. I am always willing to make new friends.
